Vencia and Xephia (universe)

Status: Rough ideas

Where Viruses roam below the surface, Vencia is waiting...

In this setting, humankind has advanced into the
future and Earth is on the verge of breaking down from
overpopulation. After recent discoveries of another
planet that could be habitable for humankind,
scientists from all around the world build starships
to send the younger population off to the planet, Xephia.

A scientist by the name of Vencia hates the species of
man, and disgusted by their ways, he created a “virus”
that feeds off of humans slowly. After a certain period
of time, the human will slowly become unresponsive until
it dies, the virus on the other hand thriving off of the
nutrients it’s consuming. When the human dies, the virus is
finally free from having to leech off of a human to be able to
survive, so now it’s independent. The end goal for viruses was
to eliminate the chance of humankind surviving and allow viruses
to thrive, for they’re superior of course. At least to Vencia.
Vencia, while he grows his ALPHA Viruses, develops a way for himself
to mutate into a virus so that he can thrive amongst his creations
on Xephia when he mass massacres the children on board every starship.
